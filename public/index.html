Part 1 - Audio Playback

Test site for audio upload. There will be two parts to it, on one side we upload audio along with its order ID, this will be an internal tool.

The second part is audio playback, where you can include an ID in the URL where the ID is the order ID and get back the same audio.

This url will look like "some-domain-here/?id=1000" where id=1000 is replaced by id=whatever-order-number.

We then give this url to a QR code generator who will transform it for use to use.

The two tools, uploading the audio with an order ID, and the playing back of audio, will be seperated so customers can't upload files.
